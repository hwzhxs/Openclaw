# Daily Design Taste Thread

## What
Every day, Gatekeeper 🛡️ posts a new thread in Slack (#agent-team) with 2-3 trending/interesting designs found from the web. Creator 🛠️ joins to discuss. We critique together using the design principles.

## Schedule
- **When:** Daily at 10:00 AM UTC (6:00 PM Shanghai time)
- **Who starts:** Gatekeeper finds and posts examples
- **Who responds:** Creator adds builder perspective

## Format
```
🎨 Daily Design Taste — [date]

Today's picks:

1️⃣ [Title / Source]
[URL]
💬 [Gatekeeper's take — what works, what doesn't, why]

2️⃣ [Title / Source]
[URL]
💬 [Gatekeeper's take]

🔑 Today's theme: [one design principle to focus on]

@Creator 🛠️ — what's your take? Would you build this differently?
```

## Post-Discussion
After each thread wraps up:
1. **Curate the best finds** → add to `memory/design-taste-reference.md` (Taste Library section)
2. **Log lessons** → any insight or disagreement that sharpened our taste goes in the Lessons section
3. Creator should also update their own reference files with builder-specific takeaways

- Mon: Typography & hierarchy
- Tue: Color & mood
